About the Temple

Shri Khatu Shyam Mandir in Parikshitgarh, Meerut, is a center of faith where devotees gather daily to seek blessings, participate in bhajans, and celebrate spiritual festivals with devotion.

Spiritual Heritage

The temple is dedicated to Lord Khatu Shyam Ji, revered as the deity of faith, humility, and fulfillment of sincere prayers. The sacred atmosphere inspires inner peace and positive energy.

  • Daily darshan and devotional worship.
  • Festive celebrations with vibrant bhajan sandhya.
  • Community-driven seva and spiritual outreach.
