About the Temple

Shri Khatu Shyam Mandir in Parikshitgarh (Meerut) is a spiritual landmark where devotees gather with faith, seek blessings, and celebrate divine traditions with unity and devotion.

Devotion, Discipline & Divine Blessings

The temple is dedicated to Lord Khatu Shyam Ji, revered for compassion and fulfillment of sincere prayers. Daily darshan, bhajans, and festivals create a deeply uplifting spiritual atmosphere.

Located in Parikshitgarh, Meerut, the mandir serves as a sacred center for families and devotees from nearby regions.

✨ Regular puja, bhajan and satsang programs
🙏 Welcoming environment for all devotees
📍 Easy access in Parikshitgarh (Meerut)
